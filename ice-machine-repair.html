Ice Machine Repair Services

Burst production needs answers in hours, not days. We respond with OEM parts, sanitation kits, and the know-how to keep your guests in ice.

Restaurants, cocktail bars, boutique hotels, medical operatories, and residential entertaining spaces count on AC/DC for clean ice every day. We maintain cuber, nugget, flake, and gourmet machines from Hoshizaki, Scotsman, Manitowoc, Ice-O-Matic, U-Line, Marvel, Sub-Zero, and True Residential.

Diagnostics & production recovery

We troubleshoot both refrigeration and water systems to stabilize production quickly.

Common ice machine problems

  • Low or no ice production
  • Slushy harvest or thin cubes
  • Freeze cycle too long / harvest alarm
  • Scale on evaporator causing bridge failures
  • Dirty water system or biofilm in bin
  • Leaking water inlet valves or pumps
  • Overheated compressors and clogged condensers

We carry relays, pumps, water inlet valves, distribution tubes, bin thermostats, float switches, and clean condenser cores on-site.

Deep cleaning & sanitation

Health departments expect NSF-grade sanitation. So do we.

Cleaning visit includes

  • Descale evaporator, distributor, and reservoirs
  • Disinfect bin, door gaskets, and scoop storage
  • Inspect filtration, replace cartridges, log pressure
  • Brush condenser coils and verify fan speed

Every visit ends with a production test, temperature reading, and documentation you can keep for audits.

Preventive maintenance plans

Quarterly or bi-annual plans keep scale from strangling production.

What we monitor

  • Incoming water quality and filtration schedule
  • Compressor amp draw, head pressure, and subcooling
  • Harvest time, cube thickness, and bin thermostat
  • Sanitary handling procedures for staff

We leave a maintenance log inside the machine so your team and inspectors know when service occurred.
